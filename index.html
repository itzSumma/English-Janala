    Frequently Asked Questions

    1. How can I start learning English on this website?
    You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
    2. Is this website free to use?
    Yes! Basic lessons and vocabulary tools are completely free. Premium features are available with a subscription.
    3. Do I need to create an account?
    No account is required to browse lessons. However, creating an account lets you track your progress and save your favorite words.
    4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
    Use our lesson-based vocabulary cards, practice pronunciation, and review synonyms daily to build a strong vocabulary over time.
    5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
    Yes, upon completing a full course, you will receive a downloadable certificate of completion you can share on your profile.
